Our Story
Tucked into a quiet corner of Via Roma in the heart of Trastevere, Caffe Via Roma has been serving authentic Italian coffee to locals and travelers since 1962. Our baristas still prepare every espresso the traditional way, our cornetti are baked fresh each morning, and our doors have always been open to anyone who wants to sit, sip, and feel the rhythm of Roman life.
